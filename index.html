Why GIMS

In a short span of time, GIMS has become the most sought after name for post-graduation in the management field. Fostering the entrepreneurial intent, focusing on delivering a holistic and futuristic education, GIMS is approved by AICTE and accredited by HLACT, UK (the first institute in the Northern Part of the country and the only institute in NCR). We are proud to have the academic partnership with the IIM, Bangalore for the certification courses on contemporary business topics.Be a part of the GIMS family and experience the difference in teaching learning system and discover the personal and professional benefits of partnering with us.
